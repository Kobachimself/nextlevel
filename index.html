KobacEvents: Event Plugin for 1.8-1.20

The commands such as /msg, /tpall, /alert, /start, /status, /pvp (On/Off), /blockdestroy (On/Off), and /placeblock (On/Off) provide players with enhanced control and interaction within the game.
